Nike Paris Tuned City - For the 20th anniversary of the Nike Air Max Plus we designed an interactive experience to discover some of the most iconic models of the last 20 years. The street name of the Air Max Plus in France is “la requin” (the shark) so for each models we created a 3D shark incarnation that shares its design features.
